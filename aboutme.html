About Me

Hi, I’m Indah — a passionate multimedia education student with a creative heart and a story to tell. As a fourth-semester student majoring in multimedia education, I’ve discovered the power of creative expression and visual storytelling. My love for singing and photography is more than just a hobby — it’s how I connect with people, express emotions, and turn everyday moments into meaningful experiences. Growing up in the beautiful Riau Islands has shaped my artistic vision and deepened my appreciation for nature and authentic stories. I truly believe that creativity is a powerful tool for education and positive change, and I’m committed to using my skills to inspire others. Let’s create something meaningful together.
